These are optional, but UGC gets more believable when the AI has real references for the product, person, and phone-camera environment.

🤳 The Creator

🛁 The Room

Bathroom, kitchen, desk, gym — the world the phone lives in.

Pro move
A creator reference plus a room reference keeps the same person in the same place across shots. That continuity is what makes UGC ads feel real instead of stitched together.
